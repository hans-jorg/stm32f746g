Adjust parameters for compilation
=================================

Use minimal newlib
    --specs=nano.specs

For linker
    -nostartfiles
    -nodefaultlibs
    -nostdlib
    -gc-sections

For compilation
    -ffunction-sections
    -fdata-sections
    -fno-builtin
